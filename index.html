About

I'm an Electrical Engineering graduate from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (May 2025) currently conducting research at USC's AI Institute (AIISC) on neuro-symbolic AI frameworks for explainable financial time-series analysis, and at IIT Roorkee's SafeTrip Lab on few-shot anomaly detection for traffic safety.

My work sits at the intersection of interpretable AI, multi-agent systems, and causal reasoning — building systems that don't just perform but can explain why. I believe the next frontier of AI is not raw capability but trustworthiness.

Previously a Design & Analysis intern at Fluor Daniel (EPCM engineering consultancy) and a research intern at Thapar, where I built a real-time drowsiness detection system deployed on Jetson Nano with 90% detection accuracy on a custom 18,000-frame dataset.
